## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I play BLOQR?
Drag and drop blocks onto the 8x8 grid. Complete full rows or columns to clear them and score points. The game ends when no more blocks can be placed.

### How do I save my progress?
Your game automatically saves when you exit to the main menu or close the app. When you return, you can continue from where you left off.

### How do I change the theme?
Go to Settings from the main menu and tap the Theme toggle to switch between Neon Blue, Neon Pink, and Wooden themes.

### How do I compete on leaderboards?
Make sure you're signed into Game Center on your device. Your high scores are automatically submitted to the global leaderboard.

### Can I watch an ad to continue playing?
Yes! When the game ends, you have the option to watch a short video ad to revive and continue playing.

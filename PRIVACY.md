# Privacy Policy for BLOQR

**Last updated: January 2026**

## Introduction

BLOQR ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Game Data:** High scores and game progress are stored locally on your device.
- **Game Center:** If you choose to use Game Center, your scores may be shared with Apple's Game Center service for leaderboard functionality.
- **Advertising Data:** We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized ads.

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.
- We do not require account creation or login.
- We do not access your contacts, photos, or location.

## Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

### Google AdMob
- Purpose: To display advertisements
- Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### Apple Game Center
- Purpose: Leaderboards and achievements
- Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

## Data Storage

All game data (scores, settings, progress) is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms. We do not transmit this data to external servers.

## Children's Privacy

BLOQR does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages and contains no inappropriate content.

## Your Rights

You can:
- Delete all local game data by uninstalling the app
- Opt out of personalized ads in your device settings
- Disable Game Center in your device settings

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
